$C = \dfrac {2y - x^2} {y^2}$.
$0 = \br{\dfrac {2y - x^2} {y^2}}'
= \dfrac {\br{2y' - 2x}y^2 - \br{2y - x^2} \cdot 2 y y'} {y^4}$. \\
$2y^2 y' - 2xy^2 - 4y^2 y' + 2x^2 y y' = 0$.
$\br{x^2 - y} y y' = xy^2$.
$y' = \dfrac {xy} {x^2 - y}$.
